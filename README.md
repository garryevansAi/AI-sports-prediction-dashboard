# AI Sports Prediction Bot

## Overview

AI Sports Prediction Bot is a data-driven sports analytics platform designed to identify betting opportunities through probability modeling, market analysis, line movement tracking, and player prop projections.

The system combines sportsbook odds, historical performance, team statistics, player metrics, weather conditions, matchup data, and market pricing to generate actionable predictions and identify positive expected value (EV) opportunities.

Unlike traditional sports picks services, the platform focuses on probability analysis and market inefficiencies rather than guaranteed outcomes.

## Edge Calculation

The platform compares sportsbook implied probability against model-generated probability.

Example:

Sportsbook Probability: 41%

Model Probability: 52%

Edge: +11%

When the model identifies significant discrepancies between market pricing and projected outcomes, a signal is generated.

---

## Signal Ratings

### Strong Signal

* High model confidence
* Significant probability edge
* Positive expected value

### Moderate Signal

* Moderate edge
* Favorable market conditions

### Watchlist

* Small edge
* Requires additional confirmation
